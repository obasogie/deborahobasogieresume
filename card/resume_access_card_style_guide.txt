RESUME Access Card — Color & Font Style Guide
--------------------------------------------------

Color Palette (Hex / RGB)
1) Dark Gray (card background)
   - HEX: #2E2E2E
   - RGB: 46, 46, 46

2) Black (QR strip background, text emphasis)
   - HEX: #000000
   - RGB: 0, 0, 0

3) White (primary text and QR labels)
   - HEX: #FFFFFF
   - RGB: 255, 255, 255

4) Divider Line (thin line under email)
   - HEX: #CCCCCC
   - RGB: 204, 204, 204

Typography
- Headline “RESUME”
  • Font: Serif (preferred: 'Merriweather', fallback: 'Times New Roman', 'Georgia', serif)
  • Weight: 900 (Black) or Bold
  • Size: ~22 pt (on a 3.5in × 2in card)
  • Letter-spacing: Slightly increased (+2%)

- Subhead “Access Card”
  • Font: Sans-serif (preferred: 'Inter', fallback: 'Helvetica Neue', 'Arial', sans-serif)
  • Weight: SemiBold / 600
  • Size: ~12 pt
  • Case: Title Case

- Name “Deborah Obasogie”
  • Font: Sans-serif (preferred: 'Inter', fallback: 'Helvetica Neue', 'Arial', sans-serif)
  • Weight: Bold / 700
  • Size: ~16 pt

- Email
  • Font: Sans-serif (same family as Name)
  • Weight: Regular / 400
  • Size: ~9.5–10 pt

- “Verified on Ethereum & IPFS” line
  • Font: Sans-serif (same family as Email)
  • Weight: Regular / 400
  • Size: ~8.5–9 pt

- QR Labels “GitHub”, “IPFS” (on black strip)
  • Font: Sans-serif (same family as Email)
  • Weight: Medium / 500
  • Size: ~9 pt for IPFS; GitHub slightly smaller if needed to balance
  • Color: #FFFFFF (white)
  • Alignment: Vertically centered with each QR code

Layout & Spacing
- Card size: 3.5 in × 2 in (300 DPI export: 1050 × 600 px)
- Margins (gray visible border): ~0.10–0.12 in on all sides
- Black QR strip width: ~1.35–1.45 in (right side)
- QR code boxes: ~1.0 in square each, equal padding inside the strip
- Divider line: 1 px (print hairline), color #CCCCCC, full width of text column
- Safe zones: keep all text ≥0.12 in from any edge

Font Stacks for Digital Reproduction
- Serif stack: "Merriweather", "Times New Roman", "Georgia", serif
- Sans stack: "Inter", "Helvetica Neue", "Arial", "Liberation Sans", sans-serif

Notes
- When printing at home from PNG/PDF, what you see is what will print—no automatic borders are added.
- Use the provided 300 DPI assets for crisp QR readability.
